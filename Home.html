About Me

I am Serena Munro, a firstyear student at Keene State College. I am
currently majoring in Film Production.
I've lived in New Hampshire my whole life, growing up in two homes due to
my parents' divorce. I have a twin sister, Miah, and a brother, Aiden, who
is two years younger than me.
I have been lucky enough to grow up in a very loving family, who I will
continue to stay connected with throughout and after college.
Personality wise, I am an introvert and I tend to not socialize as easily
as other people. During my time at college, my goal is to go out of my
comfort zone, and meet new people.
I have also always been an adventurous person, who loves to be outside.
